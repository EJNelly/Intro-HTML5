My Favorite Recipes

The first recipe is "Eric's Honey Baked Chicken Breast".

  1. Put uncooked chicken breasts in a baking dish.
  2. Pour red wine vinager into the dish until it is at half the height of the chicken.
  3. Slather both side of the chicken breasts in honey.
  4. Sprinkle a pinch of Italian seasoning onto both sides of the chicken breasts. For added garlic flavor add a pinch of garlic salt to both sides of the breasts or gloves into the dish.
  5. Bake the chicken at 375 degrees F for 45 minutes or until fully cooked.
  6. Enjoy!

The second recipe is Tuna Noodle Cassarole.

  1. Boil noodles on the stove.
  2. In a seperate pot heat cream of mushroom.
  3. Slowly add shredded pepper jack cheese and uncooked shredded tuna into the cream of mushroom and stir.
  4. Pour the finished noodles into a baking dish.
  5. Cover the noodles with the heated cheesey cream of mushroom tuna mix evenly.
  6. Cover with another layer of pepper jack cheese evenly.
  7. Evenly cover with a layer of Frosted Flakes.
  8. Back at 350 degrees F for thirty minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
